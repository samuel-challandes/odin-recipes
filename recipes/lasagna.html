Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is Garfield's favorite dish, and for good reason! It is a delicious amalgamation of pasta, meat, and cheese that warms the soul, especially on a cold winter day.

It is relatively easy to make as the oven does most of the work! However, it does take some time so make sure you plan ahead. Ready yourself as you are about to learn how to make the best lasagna in the world!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasnings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
